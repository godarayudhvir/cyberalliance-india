About Us

We are a team of passionate dietitians who have dedicated our careers to working with some of India's top hospitals. Our shared experience and commitment to patient care led us to create Dietance. Our mission is simple: to bring the same level of clinical expertise and personalized care directly to you, without the hospital-level red tape.

  • Clinical Expertise from Top Hospitals

    We bring the same high standards of medical nutrition therapy and patient care from India's leading hospitals directly to you. Our experience ensures that our advice is not just a trend but is rooted in a deep, clinical understanding of health.

  • Customized Plans, Just for You
