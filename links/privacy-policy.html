Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: December 30, 2025 | Effective Date: December 30, 2025

Welcome to Cyber Alliance India ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our engineering and IT services.

  • Information You Provide to Us: We collect names and email addresses only when you voluntarily submit them through our Web3 Forms (e.g., contact forms or inquiries).
  • Payment Information: Payments are processed via Razorpay. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers. Razorpay collects your payment information directly to process the transaction.
  • Automated Information: We use Umami Analytics to collect non-identifiable technical data (such as browser type and pages visited). Umami is a privacy-focused tool that does not track you across other websites or use "tracking cookies."

2. Our Tech Stack & Third-Party Providers

To run this website efficiently, we use several third-party services. Each may process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Infrastructure: Hosted via Cloudflare (Security/CDN) and GitHub (Source code storage).
  • Domains: Registered through Gandi.net and Dynadot.
  • Communication: We use Chatwoot for our live chat plugin. Information shared in chat is stored to help us assist you.
  • Navigation & Media: We use Google Maps (embedded) and YouTube (video hosting). These services may collect your IP address or use cookies if you interact with the map or video player.
  • Utilities: We use Dub.co for URL shortening and Web3 Forms for form processing.
  • Business Communication: We use Zoho Mail for secure email hosting and WhatsApp (Business API/App) for direct customer communication.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following "specified purposes" as per Indian law:

  • To respond to your inquiries submitted via mail, forms or chat.
  • To process payments for services via Razorpay.
  • To analyze website traffic (anonymously) to improve user experience.

4. Legal Basis (India DPDP Act 2023)

By using this site and voluntarily providing your data (filling out a form or starting a chat), you give your clear affirmative consent for us to process that data for the intended purpose. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Retention & Security

  • Retention: We keep your name and email only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of your request or as required by law (e.g., tax records for payments).
  • Security: Our site is served over HTTPS via Cloudflare. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), you have the right to:

  • Request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or erasure of your data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing.
